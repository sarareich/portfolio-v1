edX

I spent six months as an intern on the marketing team at edX. During my time there I designed a number of marketing materials such as case studies and social media graphics.

Learner Stories

EdX frequently shares learners' stories about their experiences using the platform on the blog. I was tasked with creating a blog image template that could be used to brand each of these stories.

Partner Welcomes

EdX partners with different universities and other institutions to provide online courses. When the company forms a new partnership, the announcement is accompanied by a blog post. This template was created to create a consistent brand for partners welcomes.

Case Studies

In order to improve marketing efforts, the edX marketing team wanted to begin creating case studies for partners and courses who had successfully marketed their courses. I designed this template to be used for future case studies. The full PDF can be viewed here.

Donations

These designs were created to encourage people to make donations to edX in order to support its nonprofit mission. The images are intended to be shared on social media channels.
