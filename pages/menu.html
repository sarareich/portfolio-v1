Menu

In my Typography class, one of our assignments was to redesign the menu of a restaurant in the Boston area. I chose to redesign the menu for Haley House, a local bakery and cafe. I found their current menu to be crowded and wanted to give the menu more space while maintaining a casual branding to match the atmosphere. You can view the PDF of the redesigned menu here.
