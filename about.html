Hi! I'm Sara and I'm a fourth year Business and Interactive Media major at Northeastern University. I'm a designer with an interest in interactive web experiences, and I enjoy being a part of the entire process — from the initial design to the final execution using code.

Previously, I have interned for the marketing and communications teams at edX and the Massachusetts Clean Energy Center. Currently, I am a teaching assistant for the Programming Basics course in Northeastern's College of Arts Media and Design, where I help students understand programming and design concepts using the Processing language.
